NOUS UNDERGROUND

NOUS UNDERGROUND (N/U) was founded in 2010 as an independent rap music label from Xi'an.

It is one of the top labels in Chinese rap that keep doing underground hip-hop. They focus more on Old-school than Trap. The founder of NU is the champion of the two-time Iron Mic Bettle Competitions. They are the pride of my city!
