Huang Xu - BOOM (黄旭)

Huang Xu (BOOM) is a well-known HipHop musicians from China. The most obvious feature of his Rap is storytelling.

He has composed many widely songs over the years. His rap is full of real talk. According to his pieces of music, we can find that life is his most important source of creative inspiration. He uses Rap to express his attitude and understanding of the world. His words are always focused on details and often contain very interesting sentences. His Flow and Lyrics have a taste of traditional street Hip-hop. He often sings about dreams, criticizes reality, praises mother, wife, and children. When listening to his music, we can see his complete life experience.
