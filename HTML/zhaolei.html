ZHAO LEI (赵雷)

ZHAO LEI is a Chinese folk singer-songwriter. Just like the boy-next-door, he always gives me the feeling of gracious, clean and warm.

At seventeen, he stopped the high school to put his heart and soul into music. At the beginning, he always sang his songs in the underpass. Four years later, he began to travel and performance around China by motorcycle with his friends. During that time, many original songs had been written. After backing to Beijing, he founded a music studio and participated in some TV shows, more and more people began to know him from that time on.
