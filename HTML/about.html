About Me

My name is Siyu Yang and I am a Chinese girl. I was born and grown up in Xi'an, one of the oldest cities in China. I finished my middle school and high school here, from Middle School Attached to Northwestern Polytechnical University. Then, I went to the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in Chengdu. I majoring Computer Science and Economic here.

From Feb 2018 to July 2018, I exchanged to the University of Auckland in New Zealand for a semester. Now, it is my second semester as a guest student here. I feel very glad to meet you guys!


I have learned to play flute since childhood. I also have got the highest level of Certificate of Art Grade Examination of China in Juvenile Singing and Flute when I was young. Now, C-pop is my favourite musical genre. May be due to the language, C-pop is not popular in non-Chinese countries. But I hope more people will feel the charm of C-pop in the future!
