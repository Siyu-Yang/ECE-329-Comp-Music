Bobby Chen (陈升)

Bobby Chen is an experienced Taiwanese singer and record producer.

Bobby has composed songs for many singers and worked as a producer. He also wrote magazine columns, published personal prose novels, music books, and directed MTV.Since the release of his first album in 1988, he has basically released a new album every year. So far, he has more than 30 individual albums and more than 10 combined albums. In December 1994, Bobby held his first new year's eve concert in Taiwan. Since then, he has hosted New Year's Eve concerts in Taipei every year for 27 years up to now.

In his pieces of music, besides focusing on thinking and expressing himself, the aria and melody are a bit wired. But that‘s why people can't help but fall in love with his music.
