New Pants Band (新裤子乐队)

NEW PANTS BAND is the most dazzling, most unique and most adventurous rock band in China today.

The band was founded in 1996 and officially released eight albums. Up to now, the New Pants Band have always stood on the cusp of the trend, leading the development of Chinese youth trend culture with the "new pants system" aesthetics, and has been praised as "the most fashionable band in China" by many foreign media. The early New Pants were deeply influenced by the New Wave and Old School Punk music, and later they were closer to the 80s Synth-Pop style. They are passionate about the oriental elements full of nostalgia, and the amazing stage performance of "Strange Power" can't be copied. As the most artistic and creative band in the Chinese rock music scene, the new pants also successfully went out of the country and into a more open world.

In the past 24 years, from the initial punk period to the present, the New Pants have been unable to use the word “band” to simply summarize. From music, movies, to paintings, installations, art exhibitions, new pants have always led the new trends of China. Different media methods create an independent and artistic aesthetic exclusive to the new pants.
