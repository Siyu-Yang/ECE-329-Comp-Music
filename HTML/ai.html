Eve Ai (艾怡良)

Eve Ai is a Chinese pop singer and songwriter.

She is the representative of the Soul of the Mandopop from Taiwan.In 2010, she won the fifth Super Idol Championship. In 2017, she won the 28th Golden Melody Award for Best Mandarin Female Singer. In 2019, she won the 30th Golden Melody Award for Best Composer. Her composition is not limited by the frame. Her lyrics are more gentle to imagine the thoughts hidden in your heart. Her pieces of music full of soul and character have made her a good songwriter.
