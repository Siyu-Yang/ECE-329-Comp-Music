Ma Jia (马佳)

Ma Jia is a chinese young tenor singer.

He has won the silver medal of the Kosovo International Vocal Competition in Italy and the gold medal in the Bel Canto of the Beijing Division of the " Golden Bell Award ". He is also the winner of the Obratsova International Opera Singer Contest in Russia. In 2018, he participated in the first season of TV vocal show "Super Vocal" as a tenor. In addition to Bel Canto, he is also very good at other music styles such as pop, rock and so on.
